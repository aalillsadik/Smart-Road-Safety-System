**Smart Road Safety System**

Smart Road safety system is a device by installing this device, you can turn your old vehicle into a modern one. It will be your best partner during your driving. It will use its intelligence to not only guide you on the path but also help you to drive with proper traffic rules. If you feel drowsy or sleepy while driving, the system is here for you to alert. It even uses owner face recognition to turn on the vehicle, so no one can steal your loving vehicle. If you have an accident, then our system will notify the nearest hospital, even your family member, with your live location. At night, driving it will automatically switch between high and low beam lights according to the need and situation.
